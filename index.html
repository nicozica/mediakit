About Ipsos iSay

Ipsos iSay is a rewards community dedicated to ensuring its members’ valued opinion makes a difference for societies, citizens, and brands.

Simply put, Ipsos iSay members can make their voices heard, and their opinion matter. This is because Ipsos iSay members participate in a variety of different research studies across a variety of different topics and for a variety of different clients. They can try new products, participate in online discussion boards or communities, and do research while shopping.

Ipsos iSay rewards its members for their valuable time and contribution. For every study Ipsos iSay members touch, they earn points that they can redeem for the reward of their choice, including gift cards, cash transfers, and charitable donations.

Ipsos iSay is powered by Ipsos, the third largest market research company in the world, present in 90 markets and employing more than 18,000 people. Ipsos’ research professionals, analysts and scientists have built unique multi-specialist capabilities that provide powerful insights into the actions, opinions and motivations of citizens, consumers, patients, customers or employees. “Game Changers” – Ipsos’ tagline – summarises Ipsos’ ambition to help their 5,000 clients to navigate more easily our deeply changing world.

Target audience

General Population (Gen Pop)

Most of our research activity covers the wide population, so our panel recruitment needs to include people from all demographic groups.

  • We expect to have a good mix of age groups (16+ or 18+), genders, and regions.

High value / Niche

Some groups are less represented and/or more in demand than others. These are:

For most countries:

  • Mothers – esp. mothers of babies 0-3 years old
  • Business to Business (Full time employees)
  • Males under 25

United States: African Americans, Hispanics, Electric Vehicle (EV) owners

Canada: Quebec residents and/or French speakers

LATAM: Lower social grades, Ages 45+

UK: Lower social grades or educational levels

Poland, Turkey, South Africa: Ages 45+

MENA: Locals (Saudi/Kuwaiti/Qatari/Emirati), Ages 45+
